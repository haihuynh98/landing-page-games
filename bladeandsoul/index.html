The Story

Enjoy hours of fully voiced encounters and stunning cinematics as you track down the villains who killed your master. Uncover hidden secrets as you meet dozens of complex characters. Develop your skills and uncover your true potential as you become a master in your own right.

The World

Immerse yourself in the dense forests, breathtaking vistas, expansive deserts, and savage wildlands of the Earthen Realm. Freely explore alluring regions, meet friends in bustling villages, and delve into dark corners where dangerous foes lie in wait.

Martial Arts

Experience Blade & Soul’s iconic action-based combat with upgraded animation and graphics. Switch seamlessly between Classic and “B&S” Combat modes as you spar with powerful opponents. The new skill extraction system allows for an unprecedented amount of customization as you master the unique skills of your class.

Dungeons

Powerful opponents threaten the balance. To defeat them, you must master your skills, learn their attack strategies, and work closely with your allies. With each hard-fought victory, you’ll earn powerful weapons, valuable accessories, and unique portraits. Explore dungeons solo or take advantage of the robust party matching system to team with up to 5 other players.

Infinite Windwalk

Windwalk is back. The stamina bar is not. Explore the far reaches of the Earthen Realm with unprecedented freedom. Scale walls, soar over mountains, and run on water in style with new, custom Windwalk animations.

Mantra System

The unique Mantra system allows you to bolster weapons and accessories to unlock powerful effects. Mantras can be earned by clearing Heroic dungeons and can be upgraded to enhance their benefits. Combine multiple Mantras to alter their effects based on your preferred stats and combat style.
