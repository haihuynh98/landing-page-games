  • Valley refresh

    Add the latest T951 and T03 assault rifles to your arsenal, or get up close and personal with the Bizon and TS5 submachine guns. Load up in one of four new rigs and bring the fight to your enemies.

  • WARRING FACTIONS ESCALATION

    Tensions are rising between Blackgold and Gresk with an assault on Northridge over a mysterious scientist. Uncover the backstory and unravel the tale, but watch your back! Who knows what’s waiting around every corner.
